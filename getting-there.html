Getting to Cousine Island

BY HELICOPTER

Getting to Cousine Island by helicopter is certainly the most luxurious & comfortable experience with quick & easy access directly to Cousine.

Bell ranger 4 seater takes 15 minutes from Mahe and 5 minutes from Praslin Island
Starting from:
€721 pp
BY PLANE

Getting to cousine island on an inner island flight to praslin is truly the ultimate way to experience the personality of the Seychelles. Guests would also be required to book cousine island’s solar catamaran across from praslin island to her angelic shores.

The flight takes 20 minutes from Mahe
Starting from:
€180 pp
BY SOLAR CATAMARAN

Getting to cousine island on cousine’s solar catamaran is incredibly serenic and a peaceful way to arrive as this solar catamaran glides silently through the waters. Cousine island’s team will be there to meet & greet you on praslin island to ensure a calm & relaxed arrival on cousine.

Transfer takes 40 Minutes from Praslin Island and can accommodate up to 10 persons
Starting from:
€180 pp
BY YACHT

Getting to cousine island on our appointed yachts is an ideal & breath taking panaramic indulgence of the seychelles perfect for the relaxed exotic traveller.

Transfer takes 2,5 hours from Mahe Island and can accommodate up to 6 persons on the Sunseeker Superhawk and 16 on the Sunseeker Manhattan
Starting from:
€2,500 pp
