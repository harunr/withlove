on shore, at sea and surrounds

On shore

Restored to its pristine natural state through a concerted conservation and restoration effort, guests can fulfill many dreams and wishes be exploring the Island and all its magical wonders and hidden treasures.

  • GUIDED NATURE WALKS
  • TREE PLANTING
  • SUNSET COCTAILS ON THE BEACH
  • CREOLE COOKING LESSONS
  • TURTLE EXPERIENCES
  • LIGNE ST BARTH SPA & YOGA DECK
  • BIRD WATCHING
  • CORAL REEF & ROCK POOL EXPLORATIONS
  • BEACH GAMES & KIDDIES EXPERIENCES

At sea

Cousine Island plays host to a wide range of exhilarating activities that will re-engage the spirit. Guests can get some fresh sea-air into their lungs by participating in the many at sea activities on offer.

  • SCUBA DIVING
  • FISHING
  • SOLAR CATAMARAN
  • SNORKELLING
  • WATERSPORT & ACTIVITIES
  • Excursions

In love

Imagine sipping champagne with your one-and-only while sharing the intimacy and comfort of a warm Jacuzzi bath, surrounded by fragrant coconut cream candles and watching the sun descending behind the blue horizon to a cast of dancing orange lights and twilight shadows over a tropical paradise to call your own. A truly idyllic setting for spontaneous romantic proposals as well as hot-blooded honeymooners looking for a bosom experience, Cousine Island offers a serene and private destination for intimate white beach weddings.

  • WEDDINGS
  • HONEYMOONS
  • ROMANTIC GETAWAYS

By air

Sightseeing tours around the Seychelles can be done by helicopter for those who want to get out of the water and into the air to take in the serenity of the Seychelles from a bird’s-eye view.

Take in the breath-taking views of the Seychelles archipelago that consist of as many as 115 islands, the majority of which are uninhabited. Get a full view of some of the larger marine life, including pods of dolphins swimming, diving and playing or the majestic whale shark cruising through the crystal clear tropical waters.
